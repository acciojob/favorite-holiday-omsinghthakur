Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical paradise located in the Indian Ocean. It's known for its stunning beaches, crystal clear waters, and luxury resorts.

  1. Snorkeling and Diving: The Maldives has some of the best snorkeling and diving spots in the world.
  2. Relax on the Beach: With over 1,000 islands, the Maldives has an abundance of pristine beaches to relax on.
  3. Island Hopping: Explore the different islands of the Maldives and experience the local culture.
  4. Visit Male: The capital city of the Maldives is home to colorful buildings, lively markets, and historical landmarks.
  5. Enjoy Water Sports:From jet skiing to parasailing, the Maldives offers a variety of water sports to try.
  6. Hedhikaa: Traditional Maldivian snacks such as fish cakes and short eats.
  7. Mas huni:A breakfast dish made with shredded smoked tuna, coconut, and onion.
  8. Gulha: Deep-fried dough balls filled with smoked tuna and coconut.
  9. Garudhiya: A clear fish broth served with rice and lime.
  10. Boshi mashuni: A salad made with chopped banana flower, tuna, and coconut.
